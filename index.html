Purpose

The BTC/USDT token is intended for:

  • wrapped-asset research (similar to WBTC or synthetic BTC),
  • DEX liquidity and AMM behavior experiments,
  • price discovery analysis using shallow liquidity pools,
  • wallet chart-indexing behavior testing (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet),
  • DeFi analytics and volatility modelling.

How to Add BTC/USDT to MetaMask

  1. Open MetaMask and select Ethereum Mainnet.
  2. Click Import Tokens → Custom Token.
  3. Paste the contract address:
    0xd5a90C85555Ee13863EE2b7a07D4B8807218Ef6b
  4. Ensure Decimals = 8.
  5. Confirm the import.

DeFi & Liquidity Usage

BTC/USDT can be paired on DEX platforms such as Uniswap v2/v3 with:

  • WBTC,
  • WETH,
  • USDC.

These pairs allow researchers to observe on-chain price formation, trading volume arbitrage dynamics, and chart generation in external aggregators (CoinGecko, CMC).
