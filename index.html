Corporate Context & Purpose

BTC/USDT is issued by WEBBOND LTD as a corporate token to gradually connect traditional financial assets and banking relationships with decentralized liquidity and digital asset infrastructure. The token is envisaged as a long-term instrument to position the company as an active participant in crypto-liquidity markets.

The company’s existing and potential client base may, subject to applicable internal policies and regulations, participate in the expansion of the BTC/USDT liquidity pool and support the token’s further deployment on additional networks, combined with future cross-chain bridge solutions.

  • Digitization of part of WEBBOND LTD’s asset structure through an on-chain token
  • Use of BTC/USDT in liquidity pools with established crypto-assets (e.g. WBTC) on major DEX platforms
  • Creating a transparent, on-chain layer for company and client participation in liquidity growth
  • Future issuance of BTC/USDT on multiple networks and connection via bridges
  • Building a foundation for potential integration with institutional-grade DeFi products

Liquidity & Market Positioning

The BTC/USDT token is designed to be paired with leading crypto-assets such as WBTC, WETH, and stablecoins on decentralized exchanges. As liquidity gradually increases, the company intends to use BTC/USDT as a core element of its digital market presence, while exploring additional chains and cross-chain bridges to widen accessibility and trading options.

Over time, this structure may enable the company to offer more advanced on-chain products to its clients, maintaining a clear link between off-chain banking relationships and on-chain liquidity.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1 · 2025
    Corporate tokenization and initial deployment. BTC/USDT issued on Ethereum, structured in connection with the company’s banking relationships. Initial liquidity pool created on DEX platforms with BTC-correlated assets.
  • Phase 2 · 2025–2026
    Liquidity growth and client participation. Gradual onboarding of WEBBOND LTD clients and partners into liquidity provision frameworks, while refining internal policies, reporting routines, and audit readiness for reserve- and guarantee-linked structures.
  • Phase 3 · 2026
    Multi-chain expansion and bridging. Evaluation and potential deployment of BTC/USDT on additional networks (e.g. BNB Chain, Polygon, TON), together with cross-chain bridge solutions to maintain a unified asset identity across ecosystems.
  • Phase 4 · Ongoing
    Strategic positioning in global crypto-liquidity. Building relationships with exchanges, institutional desks, and infrastructure providers to establish WEBBOND LTD as an active participant in digital asset markets, while continuously improving risk management and transparency practices.
